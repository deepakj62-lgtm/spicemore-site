ABOUT US

Spice More Trading Company took roots in the year 2010. Our Management has spent over two decades in farming, trading, and marketing spices prior to our inception. We became fully operational in 2011 with state-of-the-art processing units and storage facilities. Our efforts are dedicated to the promotion of locally produced Indian spices which have an unblemished and enviable reputation globally.
